What Does a Podiatrist Do?

Podiatrists are medical specialists trained to diagnose, treat, and prevent a wide range of foot and ankle conditions. While many general practitioners can treat basic foot pain, a podiatrist in NYC is uniquely equipped to address more complex issues, including injuries, deformities, infections, and diseases that affect the feet and lower legs.
